Who we are

A spirit-filled church for everyday people.

Mustang Creek Community Church is a spirit-filled church under the Assemblies of God in Forney, Texas — a place where found people find people, and no one walks alone.

What we live by

In This House.

Four things shape everything we do.

🏠

In This House

You belong here. This is a family — a house where you're welcomed, known, and loved before you've done a single thing.

🤝

Found People Find People

We've been found by Jesus — so we go find others. Reaching people is the heartbeat, not an afterthought.

🙌

Saved People Serve People

Grace moves us to give. Everyone has a place to serve and a gift the body needs.

🌱

We Never Stop Growing

There's always a next step — in faith, in community, in purpose. We keep moving forward together.

What we believe

Jesus at the center. The Spirit at work.

We're a Bible-believing, spirit-filled church under the Assemblies of God. We believe the Gospel changes everything, the Holy Spirit is alive and active today, and that real life is found in following Jesus together.

Our full statement of faith follows the Assemblies of God's Fundamental Truths. full statement coming

Encounter Sunday

Once a quarter, we set aside a Sunday (11 AM) for extended worship and prayer for miracles — space to encounter God without watching the clock.

It's one of the most powerful mornings in the life of our church. Come expectant.
