First time?

Here's what a Sunday looks like.

Come about 15 minutes early — grab a coffee from The Coffee Bar, check your kids into Kid City, and let someone say hello. Services run around 90 minutes: contemporary worship, then teaching from Pastor Robert or one of our pastors, and time to respond and pray. No dress code, no pressure — just come as you are.
