Case study 001 · Stan store + quiz + email flow

From giveaway winner to a system she owns

A nurse educator's link-in-bio store became a lead-capturing system. Store, quiz, and email flow: paid on a Thursday, live by Friday night.

The client

Dr. Rhonni Easton, DNP

A nurse educator helping other nurses build income beyond the bedside. A real audience, and a Stan store that wasn't doing her work justice.

The problem

A wall of tiles, catching nothing

Every product wore the same text-heavy graphic. No lead capture anywhere, and no email automation behind any of it. Every visitor either bought on the spot or vanished — and she kept none of them.

The build

Four pieces, one system

01

Store restructured

Clean-luxury look with question-led sections ("Struggling in the DMs?") so visitors route themselves to the right offer.

02

Free guide up top

Her first lead capture, sitting at the top of the store and delivering automatically.

03

A two-path quiz

"Which Online Income Path Is Right for You?" It routes visitors to their next step and sends every lead straight to her inbox.

04

A welcome flow in her voice

Three emails, triggered by the guide, written the way she actually talks.

The design rule

She owns everything. The leads go to her inbox, the list is hers, nothing routes through us. That was the requirement before a single tile moved.
