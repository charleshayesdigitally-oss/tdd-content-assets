My story (the real one)

On paper, life looked pretty good.

I'm 59. I've been married more than 23 years, I'm a cat mom, and I spent over two decades building a career in meeting planning, hotel sourcing, contracts, and events. By most measures, I'd made it.

But like a lot of women in their 50s, I started asking bigger questions. What does retirement actually look like? Do I have enough saved? What if I want more freedom — but I'm not ready to stop working?

Then life made the questions urgent. I watched my mom battle dementia and need around-the-clock care. I went through health challenges that changed how I see time, and what really matters.

That's when I realized I didn't just want one source of income. I wanted options.

I wanted flexibility — something I could build from my phone, from home, while traveling, or sitting by the pool. So I started learning, one skill at a time. No big following, no tech background. Just a willingness to figure it out.

Travel & Goodness is what came out of that: a place to share the guides, tools, and ideas I'm actually using to build a freer, fuller next chapter — and to help other women do the same.
