My story

I wasn’t supposed to be the “make money online” girl.

I was a cheer mom. Snack schedules, carpool, away games, the whole thing. I had a corporate job I thought was “safe” — right up until the day they let me go.

No backup plan. No savings runway. Just a calendar full of practices and a knot in my stomach about how we’d cover it all.

So I gave myself one shot at building income that fit around my kids — instead of squeezing my kids around a job.

I learned digital marketing in the cracks of my day. Early mornings. The bleachers. The pickup line. And it worked — I built roughly six figures in my first 6–9 months online. (That’s my real story, not a promise of yours — results always depend on the work you put in.)

Now? I teach other moms the exact thing I had to figure out alone.
