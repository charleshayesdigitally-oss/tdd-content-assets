First time here?

We saved you a seat.

Walking into a new church can feel like a lot. Here's exactly what to expect — so the only thing you have to bring is you.

A Sunday at Mustang Creek

Come as you are.

  • 1

    Arrive ~15 minutes early. Grab a coffee from The Coffee Bar and let someone say hello.

  • 2

    Check in your kids. Kid City has secure, barcode check-in and background-checked volunteers — they'll be safe and they'll have a blast.

  • 3

    Worship. Services run about 90 minutes — contemporary worship, then teaching from Pastor Robert or one of our pastors.

  • 4

    Respond. There's always space to pray and take a next step. No pressure, ever.

Good to know

Your questions, answered.

What should I wear?

Whatever's comfortable. You'll see jeans, boots, and everything in between. Come as you are — really.

What about my kids?

Camp Creek Kids (0–12) has secure check-in and a safe, fun environment. Students meet Wednesday nights too.

How long is a service?

About 90 minutes — worship, a relevant message, and time to respond and pray.
