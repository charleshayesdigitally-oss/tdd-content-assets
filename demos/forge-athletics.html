006 / Straight answers

Questions, answered.

I'm completely out of shape. Is this too advanced?+
No. Phase 1 (Rebuild) exists precisely for this. It re-establishes the basics and your work capacity before anything gets heavy. You start where you are, not where you wish you were.
What equipment do I actually need?+
A barbell or dumbbells and somewhere to do the work — a basic home setup or any commercial gym. Built-in exercise swaps cover limited equipment, so you're not stuck if you're missing a machine.
I genuinely only have three short blocks a week. Enough?+
That's the entire design. Three 45-minute sessions a week is the program — not the minimum, the plan. It's engineered to get strong on the time a working dad actually has.
What if it doesn't work for me?+
Train the program for 30 days. If you're not measurably stronger and you don't feel the difference, email for a full refund. The risk sits with us, not you.
